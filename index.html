Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Earth Tag VR processes, why it’s needed for gameplay, and how you can contact us with questions.

1. Introduction

Earth Tag VR ("the Game", "we", "our", "us") is developed and maintained by ThatoneLem. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and handling personal data responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we process, how it is used, and the rights available to users of Earth Tag VR.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required for the core functionality of the game. The types of information processed include:

  • Username: Used to identify players during gameplay and multiplayer. Usernames are provided through the Meta platform services for in-game identification.
  • IPv4 Address: Used for matchmaking and stable multiplayer connectivity. IP addresses are not used for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.
  • Microphone Audio (Voice Chat): Enables real-time voice communication between players. Voice data is transmitted live and is not recorded or reviewed.

3. Third-Party Services

Earth Tag VR relies on third-party services to deliver multiplayer and networking functionality. These services may process limited data as necessary to perform their functions. Services used include:

  • Epic Games multiplayer services
  • Mirror Networking
  • PlayFab

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policies and data handling practices.

4. Use of Information

The information processed is used solely for:

  • Establishing and maintaining multiplayer connections
  • Displaying player usernames within the game
  • Enabling real-time voice communication between players

5. Data We Do Not Collect

Earth Tag VR does not collect or process:

  • Email addresses
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Real names or personal identification details
  • Payment or billing information
  • Precise location data
  • Recorded voice data

6. Data Retention

Personal data is not retained beyond what is necessary for gameplay. Network-related data, such as IP addresses, is used for connection purposes during gameplay sessions and is not permanently stored by Earth Tag VR. Voice chat audio is transmitted in real time and is not stored.

7. Children’s Privacy

Earth Tag VR does not knowingly collect personal data beyond what is required for basic gameplay functionality. Access to the game is governed by platform-specific age requirements and parental controls provided by Meta and other platform providers.

8. Your Rights

You may stop all data processing by discontinuing use of the game. If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the development team using the details below.

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the Earth Tag VR development team via our official Discord server: discord.gg/earth-tag-1274133275421704326
